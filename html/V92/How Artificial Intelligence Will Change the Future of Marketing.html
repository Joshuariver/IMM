How Artificial Intelligence Will Change the Future of Marketing

Davenport · Guha · Grewal · Bressgott | Journal of the Academy of Marketing Science (2020) 48:24–42

Conceptual Paper 3D AI Framework Task Automation Context Awareness Human Augmentation

📄 STEP 1 — Abstract 번역

ENGLISH

In the future, artificial intelligence (AI) is likely to substantially change both marketing strategies and customer behaviors. Building from not only extant research but also extensive interactions with practice, the authors propose a multidimensional framework for understanding the impact of AI involving intelligence levels, task types, and whether AI is embedded in a robot. The authors suggest AI will be more effective if it augments (rather than replaces) human managers.

한국어 번역

미래에 AI는 마케팅 전략과 고객 행동을 크게 변화시킬 것이다. 본 논문은 지능 수준(intelligence levels), 과제 유형(task types), 로봇 탑재 여부(robot embodiment)라는 세 차원을 통합한 다차원 프레임워크를 제안한다. AI는 인간 관리자를 '대체'하기보다 '보완(augment)'할 때 더 효과적임을 제안한다.
